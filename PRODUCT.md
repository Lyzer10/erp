# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Tanzanian business administrators, finance managers, HR professionals, storekeepers, and cashiers managing daily enterprise operations.

## Product Purpose
A clean, premium, and professional ERP platform custom-tailored for East African business workflows (Tanzania), supporting English and Swahili with robust invoice, ledger, payroll, stock transfer, and bank reconciliation tools.

## Brand Personality
Clean, professional, premium, and trustworthy, anchoring on the primary brand green (`#1f9c88`) and using modern typography (Outfit font) for a clean visual hierarchy.

## Anti-references
- Over-rounded card borders (radius exceeding 16px).
- Saturated beige/cream backgrounds.
- Excessive sidebar menu bloat (we keep navigation minimal and embed sub-features inside pages).
- Cluttered SaaS dashboard widgets.

## Design Principles
1. **Contextual Integration**: Keep the sidebar clean and simple. Place sub-modules, directories, and advanced settings inside page-level tabs and dialog modals.
2. **First-Class Swahili/English Accessibility**: Localized Swahili terms must feel natural, concise, and clean, maintaining identical visual weight to English terms.
3. **Data Clarity & Integrity**: Maintain complete clarity on financial columns (TIN, VRN, VAT, transaction reference, type) and product status indicators without visual noise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Double-A contrast standards for text and inputs.
- Clean support for standard layout scaling.
- prefers-reduced-motion fallback for animations.
